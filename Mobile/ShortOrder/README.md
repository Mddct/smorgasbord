Short Order!
============================

Short Order! is a crowdsourced restaurant wait time app developed as a product for a creative design course I took while at Rose. Wireframes are done in [OmniGraffle](http://www.omnigroup.com/omnigraffle/), and [Firebase](www.firebase.com) is used as a back end.
